Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how MyTube collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Effective date: October 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect details like your name, email, and password.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you use the platform, such as watch history, search queries, and likes.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your browser, IP address, and device type.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the MyTube experience.
  • Improve our platform’s performance and security.
  • Send updates, recommendations, and important notifications.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse.

3. Cookies and Tracking

MyTube uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience and analytics. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work properly if disabled.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share information with trusted partners who help operate the platform, comply with legal requests, or protect user safety.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may delete your account to remove most personal information from our systems.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and update your account information anytime.
  • Request deletion of your account or data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.

7. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your information. However, no system is fully secure, and you use MyTube at your own risk.

8. Children’s Privacy

MyTube is not intended for children under 13 years old (or older if required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any major changes will be communicated through the website or email notifications.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@mytube.example.
